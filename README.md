### New Concepts
**Algorithm vs. Model**  
***Algorithm***​ is the recipe​ or the set of instructions.  
***Model***​ is the result​ of following the recipe with specific ingredients.  
The algorithm​ is the ***process***; the model​ is the ***product***.
